Gloria Shi

About

Gloria Shi is a first year architecture student at Cornell University who has too many interests she also wants five more minors. She is currently interested in combining her passions in architectural design and programming to further her design career.

Born in Vancouver, British Columbia, grew up in Shenzhen, China and currently resides in Seattle, Washington, Gloria often struggles to answer the question “Where are you from?”. Nevertheless, she immensely enjoys living in different countries and experiencing different cultures and wishes to take a year off and travel the world.

In her spare time, Gloria enjoys painting, crafts, photography and most significantly, reading YA novels. Gloria loves spending time with kids and have worked as a babysitter and tutor for over four years.
